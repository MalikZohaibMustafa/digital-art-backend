Welcome to the Backend of Digital Art

This API provides endpoints to generate digital art, initiate payments, and more. Below are the available endpoints and their descriptions:

POST /digitalart/text-to-art

Generates digital art based on a text prompt and specified diffusion inference steps.

{
  "prompt": "A serene landscape with mountains in the background and a river in the foreground.",
  "diffusionInferenceSteps": 1
}

POST /payment

Initiates a payment session for purchasing digital art.

{"token": "tok_visa"}

POST /paymentpro

Initiates a premium payment session for purchasing digital art.

{"token": "tok_visa"}
